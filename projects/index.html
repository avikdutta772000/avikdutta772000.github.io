  • Graph-based Policy Network for Pseudo-Relevance Feedback

    [Report]

    Used Pseudo-Relevance Feedback technique for retrieving relevant documents from a corpus for a given user query, using Indri Search Engine. The framework used REINFORCE algorithm to train a GNN-based (GAT and EGNN variations) policy network design, which gave an improvement of ~5.10% Mean Average Precision over a traditional DNN based policy network design proposed by Montazeralghaem et. al. The entire vocabulary of the corpus formed the graph nodes, and the edge weights were obtained by taking a pre-trained neural text-embedding. This idea introduced the semantic relation among different words to play some part in the retrieval, which earlier efforts neglected.

    Nov, 2022 reinforcement-learning  graph neural network  information retrieval 

  • Explainable Bayesian Machine Learning

    [Report]

    Compared the explainability of a vanilla CNN architecture by keeping the model parameters deterministic and probabilistic. Used Lime library to obtain heat maps for image explanation. For probabilistic technique, we used Bayesian Inferential techniques to obtain a distribution on the model parameters. Taking the weighted average of the lime results qualitatively summarised that deterministic approaches often are stringent and probabilitics approaches may show areas of doubt when classifying an image.

    July, 2022 Explainable AI  Bayesian Inference  Convolutional Neural Network 

  • Cheque Classification using OCR

    Used an Optical Character Recognition technology to obtain textual representation of cheque images. This information was assimilated and heursitics were designed which helped identify Balance Transfer cheques and their destination bank of transfer.

    Aug, 2023 spatial heuristics  SQL 

  • Automated Customer Call service through TTS and RVC

    Implemented an automated call service for large scale communication of information to customers in the voice of important designations holders. Used a standard Text-To-Speech (TTS) model for obtaining an audio sample for a dynamic text. This mp3 audio sample is channeled through a trained Retrieval-based Voice Conversion (RVC) model to obtain the voice mimic of an important figure. This workflow resulted in a personalised dynamic voice message to thousands of customers, without any need for recording the actual speaker.

    Sept, 2023 Audio Wave Processing 

Android Games

This section contains projects on Game Developement done during my undergraduate years.

  • Ricksy Run

    [Link] [Code]

    A platformer game where the player has to overcome challenges by making use of two portals to travel. The blue portal is for entry, which transports the player to the position of the exit portal. A mind teaser, this game is the highest grossing app ever released by Computer Graphics Lab, IIT Kharagpur. The character and enemy motion is controlled through script The character was rigged with bones and to animate it. Inverse Kinematics was applied to give a realism to the motion of the limbs. The game UI was coordinated (ordering of levels based on their difficulty) using simple C# script.

    Dec, 2020 Unity Game Engine  C#  Inverse Kinematics Animation  UX Design
